The main trait of Monochrome Music is their ability to improvise and tailor performance to the setting. This could be very quiet, ambient and piano-heavy, or deep, progressive and electronic - serving perfectly as background music. Notes is almost exactly what you might expect from a record like this one; it’s spare, it’s lyrical, it’s unobtrusive. The piano is put into a number of different contexts, adding electronics to create ethereal, emotion-evoking tracks such as these.
